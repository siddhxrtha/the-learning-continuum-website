Frequently Asked Questions

Practical answers for parents and students on subjects, scheduling, trial classes, fees and exam support.

We offer Lower Secondary G2/G3 and Upper Secondary G2/G3 classes. Subjects include English, E-Math, A-Math, Science, Pure Chemistry, Pure Biology, Pure Physics, and combined science options. View our Programmes page.

Weekly classes are held on fixed days and times to help students establish a consistent and effective learning routine. You may view our full class schedule on our schedule page. If your preferred class slot is fully enrolled, we will be happy to place you on our priority waiting list.

Yes, we offer one complimentary trial class for each student per subject. Parents may request a free trial class through our Enquiry Form. Upon receiving your request, we will contact you to confirm the date and time of this complimentary trial lesson.

Our fees for the various levels are available on this Schedule Page.

Our tutors use structured notes, guided examples, timed practice, and targeted feedback. We focus on concept mastery first, then exam accuracy and speed. Learn more about our Teaching Philosophy.

If a student is unable to attend a class due to a valid reason, we will provide the worked solutions for the missed lesson and waive the lesson fee by offsetting it against the student’s fees for the following month.

If a class falls on a designated public holiday, our teachers will endeavour to arrange a make-up lesson on an alternative day and time that can best accommodate the majority of students in the class.

Students complete exam-style worksheets and revision papers throughout the year, with additional intensive support before major school exams and national examinations.
