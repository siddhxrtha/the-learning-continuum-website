Frequently Asked Questions

Practical answers for parents and students on subjects, scheduling, trial classes, fees and exam support.

We offer Lower Secondary G2/G3 and Upper Secondary G2/G3 classes. Subjects include English, E-Math, A-Math, Science, Pure Chemistry, Pure Biology, Pure Physics, and combined science options.

Weekly classes are fixed to ensure continuity. You can view current times on our schedule page. If your preferred slot is full, we can place you on the priority waiting list.

Yes, trial classes are available for most subjects. Parents can request a trial through the enquiry form. We will confirm class availability and recommend the best starting group.

Fees vary by level and subject. A detailed fee breakdown is shared during consultation. Registration is confirmed after slot reservation and administrative details are completed.

Our tutors use structured notes, guided examples, timed practice, and targeted feedback. We focus on concept mastery first, then exam accuracy and speed.

Students complete exam-style worksheets and revision papers throughout the year, with additional intensive support before major school exams and national examinations.
